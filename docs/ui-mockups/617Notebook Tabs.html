Notebook Tabs

A molecular component for organizing content into switchable panes. Ideal for complex forms in Odoo, such as the Trip or Vehicle details screens.

Vehicle Service History

This section contains a chronological log of all maintenance and service activities performed on the vehicle. Each entry includes details such as service date, type, cost, and odometer reading. This information is crucial for tracking the vehicle's total cost of ownership and scheduling future preventative maintenance.

Compliance Documents

Here you will find all uploaded compliance documents for the vehicle. This includes, but is not limited to, the Registration Certificate (RC), Insurance Policy, and Fitness Certificate. Each document is listed with its type and expiry date to facilitate proactive renewals.

Internal Notes

This is a space for internal notes and comments related to the vehicle. Dispatch Managers, Admins, or other authorized personnel can add remarks about the vehicle's condition, specific operational constraints, or any other relevant information that doesn't fit into a structured field.

Component Variations

Variant Name: "horizontal_tabs"

The standard and only variant for this component. It presents tabs in a horizontal line above the content area on wider screens. On smaller screens, the tabs may wrap to the next line or stack vertically to maintain usability, as demonstrated by this page if you shrink your browser window.
